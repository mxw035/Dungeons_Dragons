--- title: Edermath Orchard layout: local ---

Description

You head away from the Stonehill Inn. Walkling towards the north east end of town. When you get to Barthen's Provisions you notice a road going off to the left. There is a sign saying this way to Edermath Orchard. You travel this road till its end passing by the back side of the Shrine of Luck and Garaele's home. Chickens run free in the yard. You approach the Orchard and smell the beautiful smell of fresh tilled earth. Out in the front pasture is an elderly looking man. Strongly built with silver hair. You notice that he is at least part elf.
