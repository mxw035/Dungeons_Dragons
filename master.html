Description

  • The townmasters hall has sturdy stone walls, a pitched wooden roof, and a bell tower at the back. Posted on a board next to the front door is a notice written in Common. It reads: "REWARD- Orcs near Wyvern Tor! Those of a mind to face the orc menance should inquire within." The notice bears the town's seal and an indecipherable signature
